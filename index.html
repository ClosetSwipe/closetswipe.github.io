ClosetSwipe Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

ClosetSwipe (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a swipe-first marketplace for second-hand fashion. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) Information we collect

Depending on how you use ClosetSwipe, we may collect:

  • Account details: email, username, and profile information you choose to provide.
  • Listings & content: item photos, descriptions, pricing, sizing, condition, and category details.
  • Activity data: swipes, saves, searches, clicks, messages, offers, and purchase/sale activity.
  • Transaction data: order details and shipping address. Payment processing is handled by payment providers (we don’t store full card numbers).
  • Device & log data: app/site usage, IP address, device identifiers, and diagnostic events for security and performance.

2) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the ClosetSwipe service (account, listings, swiping, messaging, offers, orders, and support).
  • Personalise and improve your experience (e.g., showing relevant items based on your activity).
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and misuse, and keep the platform safe.
  • Send service-related notifications (e.g., account, security, and transaction updates).
  • Send marketing messages only where you choose to receive them (you can opt out anytime).

3) Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted third parties only as needed to operate ClosetSwipe, such as:

  • Payment providers to process payments.
  • Shipping/carrier partners to deliver items.
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, customer support) working on our instructions.
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with law or protect users and the platform.

4) Cookies & analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Our app may use analytics events to improve reliability and features.

5) Your choices & rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You can also opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6) Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, and keep ClosetSwipe safe.
