About ClosetSwipe

A swipe-first second-hand fashion app built to make resale fast, simple, and genuinely fun.

Why ClosetSwipe exists

Resale apps can feel slow and messy — endless scrolling, too many clicks, and not enough discovery. ClosetSwipe flips that into a quick, swipe-led experience that feels more like a modern social app than a marketplace.

One swipe closer to your next look

What makes it different

Swipe-first discovery

Browse items like a feed — fast decisions, low effort, high dopamine.

Save & curate

Favourite pieces instantly and organise them into custom closets.

Style-led filtering

Find your vibe quickly using tags (e.g. baggy, 90s, denim) and optional brand filters.

How it works

1

Swipe the feed

Swipe through second-hand pieces without endless scrolling.

2

Save what you love

Favourite items and build closets by style, mood, or category.

3

Find your exact style

Use tags + optional brand filtering to get to the right pieces faster.

For buyers & sellers

Buyers: discover better pieces faster, save them into closets, and filter by the styles you actually wear.

Sellers: list quickly, get seen in swipe feeds, and reach buyers who are already into your style.

Our values

Speed: less browsing fatigue, more instant discovery.

Style: a platform that feels curated, not cluttered.

Sustainability: making second-hand the easy default.

Trust: clear listings, clear expectations, and buyer/seller protections as the platform grows.
