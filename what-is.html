About ClosetSwipe

A swipe-first second-hand fashion app built to make resale fast, simple, and genuinely fun.

Why it exists

Resale can feel slow and messy — endless scrolling, too many clicks, and not enough discovery. ClosetSwipe makes it quick, visual, and satisfying.

What makes it different

  • Swipe-first discovery: browse items like a feed — quick decisions, less friction.
  • Save & curate: favourite pieces instantly and organise them into custom closets.
  • Style-led filtering: find your vibe using tags (e.g. baggy, 90s, denim) plus optional brand filtering.

How it works

  • Swipe through second-hand pieces.
  • Skip what you don’t love and save what you do.
  • Build closets by style, mood, or category.

For buyers & sellers

  • Buyers: discover better pieces faster and save them into closets.
  • Sellers: list quickly and get seen by people who actually want your style.

Values

  • Speed: less browsing fatigue, more instant discovery.
  • Style: curated feel, not clutter.
  • Sustainability: making second-hand the easy default.
  • Trust: clear listings and clear expectations.
