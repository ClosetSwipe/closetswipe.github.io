About ClosetSwipe

The Problem

Resale apps haven’t evolved in years. Scrolling through endless listings is slow, uninspiring, and transactional. While social apps like Tinder and TikTok transformed how people engage online, the second-hand fashion world remains stagnant.

The Solution

ClosetSwipe is a next-generation resale platform designed to make second-hand shopping fast, fun, and addictive.

Swipe-to-Offer: Borrowing from the swiping mechanics users already love, ClosetSwipe makes buying effortless. Swipe right to show interest and instantly make an offer, swipe left to move on.

Closets: Like Pinterest boards for fashion, Closets let users curate and showcase styles they love, turning resale into a social and identity-driven experience.
