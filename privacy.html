ClosetSwipe Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

ClosetSwipe (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a second-hand fashion marketplace with a swipe-first experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) What we collect

Account & profile

Name/username, email, password (stored securely), profile photo, and optional details you choose to add.

Listings & activity

Photos and descriptions you upload, item details (size, brand, condition), your swipes/saves, messages, and offers.

Orders & payments

Shipping address and order information. Payment details are handled by our payment provider (we do not store full card numbers).

Device & usage

Basic app/website usage data (e.g., pages viewed, clicks, app version), and approximate location from IP (not precise GPS unless you enable it).

2) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Enable buying/selling (listings, swiping, messaging, offers, orders, shipping).
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests.
  • Improve the app (including personalising your feed based on your activity).
  • Send important service emails (e.g., security, account, order updates).
  • Send marketing messages only if you opt in (and you can opt out any time).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and misuse.

3) Legal bases (UK/EU)

Where UK GDPR / EU GDPR applies, we process personal data under these legal bases:

  • Contract – to provide the service (account, listings, orders).
  • Legitimate interests – to keep ClosetSwipe safe, improve features, and prevent fraud (balanced with your rights).
  • Consent – for certain marketing messages and optional features.
  • Legal obligation – where required (e.g., tax, accounting, lawful requests).

4) Sharing your information

We share information only as needed to operate ClosetSwipe, including:

  • Payment providers to process payments.
  • Shipping/carrier partners to deliver items.
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, customer support) who process data on our instructions.
  • Other users where necessary: your public profile, listings, and messages to complete transactions.
  • Legal/safety if required to comply with law or protect users and the platform.

We do not sell your personal information.

5) Cookies & analytics

If you use our website, we may use cookies or similar technologies to keep things working and understand site performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you use our mobile app, we may use analytics events to improve features and reliability.

6) Your choices & rights

You can:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to certain processing or ask us to restrict processing.
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent).
  • Opt out of marketing at any time (using the unsubscribe link or in-app settings).

To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity.

7) Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, including legal, accounting, and safety requirements. You can request deletion, and we’ll delete or anonymise data unless we need to keep it for legitimate legal reasons.

8) Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9) Children

ClosetSwipe is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10) International transfers

If we transfer data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards (such as contractual protections) to help keep your information protected.
