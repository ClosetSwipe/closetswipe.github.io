ClosetSwipe Privacy Policy

ClosetSwipe (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a swipe-first marketplace for second-hand fashion. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as email and username
  • Listings, photos, and descriptions
  • Swipe, save, message, and transaction activity
  • Shipping and order information
  • Basic device and usage data

How we use information

  • Operate and improve ClosetSwipe
  • Enable buying and selling
  • Personalise your experience
  • Keep the platform safe
